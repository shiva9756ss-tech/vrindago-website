Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a room through VrindaGo?

Currently, bookings are available through our mobile app. You can browse rooms on this website, but to book, please download the VrindaGo app.

How can I list my property on VrindaGo?

To list your room or property, please contact us at support@vrindago.in or fill out the contact form above with "Want to List My Room" as the inquiry type.

When will the local guide feature be available?

We're working hard to launch the local guide feature soon. You can submit your interest through our guides page to be notified when it becomes available.

Are all accommodations verified?

Yes, all accommodations on VrindaGo are personally verified by our team for safety, cleanliness, and quality standards.

What areas do you cover?

Currently, we focus specifically on Vrindavan and Mathura. We're planning to expand to other spiritual destinations in the future.

How do I report an issue or provide feedback?

You can reach us at support@vrindago.in or use the contact form above. We appreciate all feedback as it helps us improve our services.

Need Immediate Assistance?

If you're currently traveling and need immediate assistance with your stay or have any urgent issues, please email us at support@vrindago.in with "URGENT" in the subject line.

We monitor our email regularly and will respond to urgent matters as quickly as possible.
